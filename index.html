Free State Smart Skills Centre

Our Vision

To empower both rural and urban youth with Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) skills, while bridging the digital literacy gap that exists in the Free State.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide learners and youth with opportunities for multi-certification programs that enhance employability, foster innovation, and nurture creativity. By equipping participants with essential 4IR skills, we aim to ensure they remain competitive in the labor market locally and globally, prepared to thrive in the evolving digital economy.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive programmes and services designed to empower individuals and organizations through skills development, training, and professional growth opportunities.

Skills Development Training

Comprehensive training programmes tailored to industry needs and learner aspirations.

  • Accredited training courses
  • Workplace-based learning
  • Technical and soft skills
  • Industry certifications

Adult Education & Training

Foundational learning programmes for adult learners seeking to enhance their qualifications.

  • Literacy and numeracy
  • Life orientation skills
  • Computer literacy training
  • Foundational skills development

Career Guidance & Counselling

Professional guidance to help individuals make informed career and educational choices.

  • Career assessments
  • Individual counselling sessions
  • Job readiness workshops
  • CV writing and interview skills

Digital Skills Training

Modern technology and digital literacy programmes for the evolving job market.

  • Computer applications training
  • Digital marketing basics
  • Social media management
  • Online collaboration tools

Entrepreneurship Development

Support for aspiring entrepreneurs to develop and grow sustainable businesses.

  • Business plan development
  • Financial management training
  • Marketing and sales skills
  • Networking opportunities
